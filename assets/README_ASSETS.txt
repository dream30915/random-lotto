Place your branding assets here:

- logo.png        Transparent PNG recommended (approx 1200px width). This will appear on the poster top-right and on the web header.
- bg.jpg          Optional background texture. Any size; will be scaled to poster.
- Kanit-SemiBold.ttf  (Optional) If missing, download.php will try to auto-download from Google Fonts.

You can replace these at any time. The app will pick them up automatically on next run.

Tip: If bg.jpg is missing, the poster generator will automatically create a decorative purple/gold background and save it here on first use. You can also manually (re)generate a larger background via dev/generate_bg.php.

- Poster params: n=123456, title=ลาวสตาร์VIP, date=09/11/68, wm=1 (watermark on share)
- The homepage dropdown and date chip feed these parameters automatically.
 - Extra params for sub-blocks: lead=9, two=97,92,93, three=191,619
 - Homepage random fills these fields; download/share include them in the poster.
